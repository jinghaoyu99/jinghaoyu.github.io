Research Interest

My research interests are in geometry, topology, mathematical physics, and related fields. My major interest is Gromov-Witten theory and related mirror symmetry. Roughly speaking, Gromov-Witten invariants are the numbers of algebraic curves of various degree and genus lying on a variety. These invariants are defined by the cohomology classes on the moduli space of curves. Because we lack enough knowledge of the geometry of moduli space, the direct computation of Gromov-Wittern invariants are difficult in general. Thus, we ask for find some other ways to do the calculation.

There are several ways to do it:

(1) The mirror symmetry links Gromov-Witten invariants of a manifold to the complex structure of its dual manifold. The phenomena of mirror symmetry are so rich that we use various phrases to describe them, for example, homological mirror symmetry, topological recursion and so on.

(2) By virtual localization formula, the invariant are reduced to the computation of Hodge integrals. The later ones are related to HOMFLY polynomials.

I am also interested in various topics of geometry and topology: like K-stability, birational geometry, TQFT.
