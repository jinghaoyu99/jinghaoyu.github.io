About me

I am a Ph.D. student at Department of Mathematics, Tsinghua University in Beijing, China. My advisor is Zhengyu Zong. From 2017 to 2021, I was an undergraduate student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China. I received my bachelor's at SUSTech in 2021.

Research Interest

My research interest concentrates on Gromov-Witten theory and related fields. The Gromov-Witten theory is a virtual counting of curves on a manifold (orbifold). It is shown that the recursion relations in GW theory can be described by topological recursion, which is a B-model in mirror symmetry. I am spending most of my time to find out explicit relations between them.
