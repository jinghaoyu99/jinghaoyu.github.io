About me

I am a Ph.D. student at Yau mathematical sciences center (YMSC), Tsinghua University in Beijing, China. My advisor is Zhengyu Zong. From 2017 to 2021, I was a undergraduate student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China. I received my bachelor at SUSTech in 2021.

Research Interest

My research interest concentrates on Gromov-Witten theory and related field. The Gromov-Witten theory, initiated from mathematical physics, is a virtual counting of curves on a manifold (orbifold). It is shown that the recursion relations in GW theory can be described by topologcial recursion, which is a B-model in mirror symmetry. I am spending most of time to find out explict relations among them. Beyond, I am interested in other mathematics, like moduli space, Schramm-Loewner evolution.
