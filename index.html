About me

I am a Ph.D. student at Yau mathematical sciences center (YMSC), Tsinghua University in Beijing, China. My advisor is Zhengyu Zong. From 2017 to 2021, I was a undergraduate student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China. I received my bachelor at SUSTech in 2021. My bachelor academic advisor is Yifei Zhu. My bachelor thesis advisor is Shaochuang Huang.

Research Interest

My research interests are in geometry, topology, mathematical physics, and related fields. My major interest is Gromov-Witten theory and related mirror symmetry. I am also interested in various topics of geometry and topology: like K-stability, TQFT, birational geometry.
