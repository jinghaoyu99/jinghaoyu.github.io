About me

Research Interest

My research interest concentrates on Gromov-Witten theory and related field. The Gromov-Witten theory, initiated from mathematical physics, is a virtual counting of curves on a manifold (orbifold). It is shown that the recursion relations in GW theory can be described by topologcial recursion, which is a B-model in mirror symmetry. Meanwhile, topological recursion links many fields, such as Hurwitz numbers, Weil-Peterson volume, HOMFLY polynomials, random matrix theory. I am spending most of time to find out explict relations among them. Beyond, I am interested in other mathematics, like moduli space, complex geometry.
