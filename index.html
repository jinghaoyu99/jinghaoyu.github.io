Research Interest

My research interest concentrates on Gromov-Witten theory and related field. The Gromov-Witten theory is initiated from mathematical physics, which is a virtual counting of curves on a manifold (orbifold). It is shown that the recursion relations in GW theory can be described by topologcial recursion, which is a B-model in mirror symmetry. Meanwhile, topological recursion links many mathematical theory, including Hurwitz numbers, Weil-Peterson volume, HOMEFLY polynomials, random matrix theory and so on. I spend most of time to find out their explict relations. Beyond, I am interested in many mathematics, like moduli space, complex geometry.
